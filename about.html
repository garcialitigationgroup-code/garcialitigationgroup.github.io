About Garcia Litigation Group

Jeffrey Garcia is the founder and lead attorney at Garcia Litigation Group. He began his career defending large companies and insurers. That experience informs his plaintiff‑side practice today—anticipating defense strategy, pressure points, and valuation models to position cases for maximum leverage.

Our philosophy is simple: prepare like every case is going to trial while maintaining candid communication with clients. We take on a limited number of matters to ensure focus and responsiveness.
